# Solana Saga — Demo Video Guide

## Video Style: Fast-Paced Trailer (60-90 seconds)

No talking head. No slow explanations. Think **game trailer** meets **product hunt launch video**.

---

## PRODUCTION CHECKLIST

### Before Recording
- [ ] Clear browser cache, fresh session
- [ ] Wallet connected with USDC + SOL
- [ ] At least 2-3 active markets ready
- [ ] Screen recording at 1080p or 4K
- [ ] Background music ready (royalty-free synthwave/electronic)

### Tools Needed
- Screen recorder (OBS, Loom, or QuickTime)
- Video editor (CapCut, DaVinci Resolve, or Premiere)
- Synthwave music track (Epidemic Sound, Artlist, or free alternatives)

## MUSIC RECOMMENDATIONS

**Vibe:** Synthwave / Retrowave / Cyberpunk

Free options:
- [Uppbeat](https://uppbeat.io/) — Search "synthwave" or "cyberpunk"
- [Pixabay Music](https://pixabay.com/music/) — Search "electronic"
- [YouTube Audio Library](https://studio.youtube.com/channel/UCxxxxxx/music) — Filter by "Electronic"

Paid (better quality):
- [Epidemic Sound](https://www.epidemicsound.com/) — Search "synthwave"
- [Artlist](https://artlist.io/) — Search "retro electronic"

**BPM:** 120-140 works well for fast cuts

---

## TEXT ANIMATION STYLE

Use these effects in your editor:

1. **Slam/Pop** — Text appears instantly with slight scale bounce
2. **Glitch** — Quick RGB split effect on transitions
3. **Typewriter** — For code snippets
4. **Fade Up** — For softer moments

Font suggestions:
- **Headers:** Bold sans-serif (Bebas Neue, Montserrat Black)
- **Code:** Monospace (JetBrains Mono, Fira Code)

Colors to match app:
- Cyan: `#00F3FF`
- Pink: `#FF00AA`
- Green: `#00FF88`
- Gold: `#FFD700`
- Dark BG: `#0a0a0f`

---

## RECORDING TIPS

1. **Smooth scrolling** — Use trackpad, not jerky mouse movements
2. **Pre-plan clicks** — Know exactly where you'll click before recording
3. **Record at 60fps** — Smoother for speed-ups
4. **Record longer, cut shorter** — Get 3-4 takes of each action
5. **Hide cursor** in final edit (or use a minimal cursor)

---

## EXPORT SETTINGS

For hackathon submission:
- **Format:** MP4 (H.264)
- **Resolution:** 1920x1080 minimum
- **Frame rate:** 30fps or 60fps
- **Bitrate:** 10-20 Mbps

For Twitter/X:
- Max 2:20 duration
- Max 512MB
- 1280x720 works well
