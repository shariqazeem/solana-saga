## ðŸŽ¥ Production Notes

### Video Style
- **Pace:** Fast-paced, energetic (like a tech product launch)
- **Music:** Upbeat electronic/synthwave (similar to Solana branding)
- **Transitions:** Smooth, quick cuts (1-2 second scenes max)
- **Text overlays:** Minimal, only for key stats

### Technical Specs
- **Resolution:** 1080p minimum (4K preferred)
- **Format:** MP4, H.264
- **Aspect Ratio:** 16:9
- **Frame Rate:** 60fps for smooth animations
- **Audio:** Clear voiceover, balanced with music

### Screen Recording
- **Tool:** OBS Studio or Loom
- **Cursor:** Enabled (shows user interaction)
- **Zoom:** Use for important UI elements
- **Speed:** 1.2x speed for browsing, normal for betting

### Voiceover
- **Style:** Enthusiastic but professional
- **Accent:** Clear, neutral (American or British)
- **Tool:** Professional mic + Audacity/Adobe Audition
- **Background:** Quiet room, no echo

### Music
- **Source:** Epidemic Sound, Artlist, or royalty-free
- **Volume:** -20dB to -15dB (background, not overpowering)
- **Sync:** Beat drops aligned with key moments (confetti!)

- Aim for voiceover at -6dB, music at -18dB
